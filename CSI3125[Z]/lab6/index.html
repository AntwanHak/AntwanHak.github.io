Our Team

Meet the Veterinarians & Team of Glen Erin Animal Hospital in Mississauga! We’re pleased to provide exceptional vet care for your pets!

Dr. Sarah Thompson

Dr. Thompson has known that she would be a veterinarian since she was 9 years old. The driving reason then and now is her love of all animals. As a consequence, over the years she has cared for and functioned as staff for many dogs, a dozen cats and a variety of birds, rabbits, hamsters, tortoises, turtles and one mouse. These personal and emotional experiences have made her an empathetic and compassionate veterinarian. Sarah works on Mondays.

Dr. Emily Sudds

Dr. Emily Sudds joined Glen Erin Animal Hospital and Meadowvale Animal Clinic in 1999 after graduating from the Ontario Veterinary College. She enjoys all aspects of general practice but is particularly interested in internal medicine, dermatology, and preventive medicine. Dr. Emily loves working with cats and dogs and especially likes getting to know her patients and their families. When away from work, Dr. Emily is usually busy spending time with her husband and two kids, as well as their kitty, guinea pig and rabbit. Emily works on Tuesdays and Wednesdays.

GDr. Leila Denstedt

GDr. Leila Denstedt told her parents at the age of 5 that she wanted to be a veterinarian. She always had pets growing up, and as soon as she turned 13, Leila started volunteering at veterinary clinics in order to pursue her goal! She continued to work at veterinary clinics throughout high school, as well as during her undergraduate degree, where she obtained her Bachelor of Science from the University of Guelph. Leila works on Thursdays and Fridays.
